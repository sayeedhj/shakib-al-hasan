Shakib Al Hasan

No.1 Allrounder

Shakib Al Hasan, also known as Saqibul Hasan, is a Bangladeshi international cricketer and widely considered to be one of the greatest all-rounders of all time. His aggressive left-handed batting style in the middle order, controlled slow left-arm orthodox bowling has made him a consistent player for Bangladesh. etc
